Hi Angie,

'Anymore' is probably the wrong word to use, 'yet'
might be better. No-S is pretty new, and until I
figured out google ads last month, not many people
knew about it. But there are a few of us here already,
and we'd be glad talk you through the first miserable
steps and beyond.

My advice to you in the beginning is don't worry if
your portions are large and your weekends excessive,
just make sure you stick to the letter of the law
during the week. Your weekends and portion sizes will
fall into line once you have the habit in place (about
3 weeks). And bug us if you're having trouble.

Good luck!

Reinhard
