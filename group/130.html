answer to your question.

Please keep two things in mind:

1) no one except for me (and Justin) has been on this
diet for very long. 

2) I think only answers from people who have been on
the diet for at least 3 weeks are meaningful, so
that's how I framed the question. But that's not a lot
of people at the moment.

3) the poll will never close, so people can change
their answers as they progress/fail miserably. This
means the poll will get more and more informative as
people try the diet.

inaccurate and subject to abuse. But I'm not aware
that anyone else is doing anything better.
