This past weekend I did an S day on Saturday, and decided yesterday,
Sunday, to no S for a change because I felt I was going overboard on the
weekend,,,erasing all my hard work during.the week..I know Reinhard says not
to do that, but I am in Menopause
and it is very hard to lose a pound, not like when we woman are younger. As

try to just avoid those things...the only sweet I love is ice cream, so that

sugarless yougart,
and lots of lean meats, fish and veges...not potatoes-however, green veges..
.lots of Salads.

no Sugar is the best part of no S, as again that is a "momment on the lips,
forever on the hips".

my diet, are lots of Stir frys....meat or Shrimp, and green veges, and
Sometimes almonds or cashews thrown in, fried in olive oil. we also avoid
deep fried and trans fatty foods.
Well this works pretty well for us.
