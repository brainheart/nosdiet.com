Hi Donna :)
The three weeks in are, in terms of No S Diet, that period of time
which is supposed to be the "hump" we must cross till the new habits
really kick in...Many people have had difficulty staying completely
on program during this period and it is the part of No S which
requires the most behavioral changes "up front"
But weighing in is not specifically "recommended" or encouraged at
the end of this three weeks...We all have different personal goals
and some people feel happy weighing once a month, once a week....
If you look at Reinhards original experiences while developing No S,
he didn't even weigh himself until about two months into the process.
You didn't miss anything. Set a realistic goal for yourself and try
to use your scale as a once in a while reality check...If you stick
with this method, over time, as long as you compliment it with
moderate exercise, you will lose weight...Slowly, but at a healthy
rate, and you will be forming habits which will last a lifetime.
Are you new?
Good luck :)
