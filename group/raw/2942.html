I am proud of my moment today so I wanted to share it with you...
Yes a minor triumph, but 365 minor triumphs a year will add up big
time.
I was really hungry for lunch before and I had to wait for my car to
be inpected.. I decided to grab a slice of pizza from this excellent
place right next door... Actually I thought of Dunkin Donuts first
because it had been a while and I forgot the pizza place was there.
I figured, after waiting for over 2 hours that I should have
*something* even if that was a muffin or croissant... but I totally
ditched the plan once I remembered there was "real" food just one
door over... so that impulse was successfully evaded! Then, hungry
as I was, I ordered 2 slices, a deeeelicious roasted pepper and
fresh mozzarella and basil slice, and then a smaller "grandma" style
slice... I was really over hungry at the time... But guess what?
I never ate the second slice because I actually felt very satisfied
with that first veggie loaded one!!! I gave the second one to my son
Richard for lunch (poor guy was screaming at me because I put hot
red pepper on it and his lips were burning! LOL)
This is a first in *history* I kid you not people... Resisting pizza
isn't something that comes naturally to me, but in this case my body
said "That's enough I'm fine now..." so I just listened!
Yay me and Yay NoS :)!!!
Hope all you are having a great day!
