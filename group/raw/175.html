I think the goal is to plan during the week what special treat or 2 you will
indulge in on the weekend. Look forward to it. Then, on the weekend, eat
your treats guilt free. For me, anyway, the weekend isn't for going all out
crazy and stuffing myself. I still stop eating before I feel that "full"
feeling. I still eat sensibly except that I add a few extra things in that
I wouldn't normally eat.

I think the planning part is key, especially if you have the tendency to go
a bit overboard.
