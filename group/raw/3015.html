On Tue, 08 Mar 2005 11:41:41 -0000, elderhillrocks
<elderhillrocks@...> wrote:
>
> I am writing an analysis on the NoSDiet and Reinhart Engels, and I
> am seeking an opinion of someone who is on the diet. I am curious
> as to what it was, if something specific, about what you read on the
> website that encouraged you to take on this diet.

I had considered giving up chocolate and other sweets before but never
stuck to that for very long.

So I liked the fact that this "diet" was really simple: no
calorie-counting or looking up numbers in tables, keep eating much the
same way you were and as much as you want, just healthier. And the
fact that it had a name and a plan made it easier to stick to.

And I must say that the S days are an important part... if I had to
give up chocolate and biscuits (cookies) forever, I'd miss them a lot,
but if I went back to them after half a year, I'd probably gain back
quickly any weight I might have lost. So I like the fact that this
diet can be a way for life and *still* let you eat sweets! You just
have to wait till the weekend :)
