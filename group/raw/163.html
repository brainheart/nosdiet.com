I've ran through a few hard times these last few weeks, working 6 days a
week, 16 hours a day for at least 4, and 8 the others doesn't promote good
eating, at least for me. I've found ways around that..Changing weekend days
to Wed and Friday, the hardest days to get through for me. Adding a couple
extra "special" days with a birthday and wedding in there. I have not lost
weight yet, at all...Possibly because I don't get in the exercise I should.
I squeeze in what I can, I carry the heavy boxes, and give the light ones to
my assistants, I stay on my feet as much as possible, but I just don't feel
the drive to do "exercieses" when I get home. Anyway, I have not bought pop
