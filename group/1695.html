Welcome, Amy.

Fast food is not off limits. You can enjoy it every
once in a while with a clear conscience. But
obviously, if you eat it every meal, no-s isn't going
to work. It's common sense, not magic.

But don't worry about fast food too much in the
beginning. Just make sure to get the literal rules
down. Once you've gone 3 weeks strict you can worry
about how much fast food you're eating. If you're a
fast-foodaholic now, my guess is the literal rules
will have a big impact without additional
intervention.

A while ago I proposed "no supersizes" as an
additional S for people who have trouble with fast
food. And remember that sugar-soda is out.

Best of luck to you and let us know how it goes,

Reinhard
