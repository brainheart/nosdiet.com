Hang in there, Maxine. I didn't weigh myself at all
for the first couple of months. It's slow, but if you
eat less (and it should be very obvious whether you're
eating less on this system because your windows of
opportunity are few and easy to monitor) you will lose
weight. If you've been strict, I'm confident results
will come. If you've had lapses but basically stuck to
your guns, you should be in a better position this
week, willpower and habit wise, to reduce such lapses.
