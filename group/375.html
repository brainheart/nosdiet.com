> My advice is: 
> 
> 1) get the cheese nips etc. out of the house. If you
> want to indulge on s-days, buy a small amount so you
> don't have leftovers to tempt you.
> 
> 2) give yourself decent sized meals so you aren't so
> hungry for snacks. Until you know just the right
> amount it takes, it's ok to make the meals a little
> too large at first. Once you have the discipline of no
> snacking in place it's relatively easy to fine tune
> the portions.
> 
> 3) if somehow #1 and #2 don't work on any given day,
> drop the crackers and nips and stick with the fruits.
> It's not officially "ok," rules-wise, to snack on
> fruit, but it's officially "much better" than the
> alternatives.
> 
> 4) Drink more water, drink tea. Drink anything except
> sugar water. It's astonishing what a difference this
> can make.
> 
> 5) bug us if none of this works
> 
> Hope this helps,
