I think the goal is to plan during the week what special treat or 2 you 
will

out
crazy and stuffing myself. I still stop eating before I feel that "full"
feeling. I still eat sensibly except that I add a few extra things in that
I wouldn't normally eat.

I think the planning part is key, especially if you have the tendency to 
go
a bit overboard.
