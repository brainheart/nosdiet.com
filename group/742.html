Subject: On resisting Easter candy
From: Reinhard Engels
Date: Tue, 13 Apr 2004 06:19:46 -0700 (PDT)
    
1) The plus side of all the sugar and artificial
what-not they pump into these things is that they
*will* keep till next weekend. As far as I can tell,
Cadbury eggs never go bad. Your atavistic cave man
urge to gorge before it rots *really* doesn't apply
here.

2) If competition from children/family rather than
paranoid fear of spoilage is your problem, tuck away a
choice stash. I know whereof I speak. My family eats
like a pack of wolves.
