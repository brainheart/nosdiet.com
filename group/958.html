Ah, you meant *drink* drinks.

As I mention on the site, I give myself a 2 drink a
day "glass ceiling." I usually have a glass of wine or
beer with dinner, sometimes two, never more. But I
don't have a rule about when. That's just usually when
I want it.

http://www.nosdiet.com/#drinks

I used to have a problem with occasional excessive
drinking so this rule is very helpful to me. I'm
amazed at how well it's worked. If it weren't *so*
simple, and I weren't afraid of encouraging alcoholics
to further self deception, I'd give it it's own site.

But from what I've read, 1 drink is the recommended
limit for women... sorry. I guess physical glass
ceilings are just as unfair as metaphorical ones.
