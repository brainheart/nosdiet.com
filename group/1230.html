This is very encouraging.

My name is Valerie and I am beginning my second week of the nosdiet. I 

found out about it through Thom on the Off-Track e-mail list.

I am 36 years old and I had the duodenal switch weight loss surgery in 
March

2001. I was 339 pounds and lost down to 196. Last year I got pregnant and

track.
