Mush,
Sound advise. I to find that many times I'm eating too little to
make noS work properly with just the 3 meals. Before noS I could eat
next to nothing all day because I would more than make up for it at
dinner and before bed. Trouble is I must have nailed 3,000 to 5,000
calories on those days.
On noS I find the need to have reasonably sized meals or my body is
yelling at me by 7:00 P.M. The weeks when the calories are about the
same for each meal my weight goes down. Other times if I'm not so
careful there may be very little loss of even gain, my ability to resist
a snack or one of my wife's home made cookies is almost non-existent.
"S days should be S days so N days aren't." When meal size is
right, you'll make it through the night.
