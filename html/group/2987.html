Welcome back. Sorry for the delay... I'm still
drowning in one mailbox. Good idea to get a dedicated
one.

I'm reminded of how much I like being on nos when
people moan to me (usually around the holidays or
after some big celebratory meal) "I have to go on a
diet." I know better, but I'm always a little
surprised. Here I am innocently enjoying some festive
meal and my neighbors tell me they can't. They're
always bouncing around from one thing to another and
back. I stick with one and find it so unobtrusive at
this point that I barely remember I'm on it. If it
weren't for this group, I'd hardly ever think about
nos. It's become that ingrained and natural. So stick
with it this time, so you can really forget about it.
