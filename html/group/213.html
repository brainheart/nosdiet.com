Hi all, I dragged the scale out today from under the clutter, and had 
a pleasant surprise. On 09/15 I weighed 368 and today I weigh 353 a

again/off-again of strict low carb and then bingeing--not very 
healthy. Any regimen too strict means being superfat for me so it 
seems. I also have been bouncing on my rebounder (2 or 3x a day for 
about 2-3 minutes each time because I have fractured both my big 
toes/confrontations with trash cans--enough said). I am very 
encouraged today that I may be able to live like a real person in the 
real world. I believe this holiday season shall be easy to get 
through with health and sanity.
