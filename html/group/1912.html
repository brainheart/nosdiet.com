Hi Donna,

Hang in there. Remember, no self-revenge. You've seen
the enemy, he got the best of you this time, but
you're still here, and you've learned from the
experience. And even now, at your low, you're just 3
weeks from building a new habit. Success is that
close.

Here's an idea: if your work makes lunch timing iffy,
be sure to give yourself an extra big breakfast to
budget for that risk. If lunch comes on time, then
lunch can be a little smaller to compensate. Keep the
focus on meal habits in the beginning, portion control
can come later.

Keep posting if you run into trouble. We wont get
tired of encouraging you.

Hoping today goes better for you,

Reinhard
