to "Go Bananas and nuts!" , and who better to post such a recipe as 

the original nut, me...(shut up Jools I know you are laughing 
yourself silly!)

Take a banana and slice it lengthwise so you have two or three 
lengthwise slabs...
Then take about one tablespoon of peanut butter, smush it on top of 
the slabs...
Then take some cocoa powder... No Sugary chocolate syrup, just the 
cocoa..
Then sprinkle some crushed pecans or almonds for some crunch and top 
with a tiny drizzling of raw honey!

MMMMMMMMMMM!!!!!!
Try it and see if it doesn't make you go bananas and nuts!
(other option is to take above recipe and then freeze it so it's
