Greetings to Kevin and all;
I haven't been a tee-totaler lately. You see, they started selling candy corn here 
and I just couldn't resist. My first bag lasted a week, and the second bag (yes,

off the seconds and most sweets and sodas, and I stop eating before I get full. It

had been haunting me for like five years. I'm only like a half pound below it, but 
I'm thrilled. No S diet does work, it's doable, and I still feel like a real person, 
not a perpetual dieter.

Best wishes for a healthy weekend.
