Just my two cents regarding breakfast. Eat what sounds good to you
when you wake-up. If you crave something sweet when you wake-up. Find
something like fruit or yogurt. Try pancakes or crepes with yogurt,
yummy. Where did we get this crazy notion that certain foods need to
be served at certain times. I love having bacon and eggs or pancakes
for dinner. I love having Blt's heavy on the lettuce and tomato or
bean burritos, for breakfast. Just make sure you are listening to
what your body is craving. within the NoS rules of course. Do your
best to make choices that best fit what your body really wants. In my
experience, I am less likely to cheat if I eat as close to what my
body wants without going against the rules. -Mel
