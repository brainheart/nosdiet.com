"Last year, we spent an estimated $46 billion on diet
products and self-help books.

Much of that money is wasted. Indeed, a government

For a moment I did a double take and thought the

eating regular food, just less of it). Hope you
converts from other systems are enjoying your extra 15
to 80 dollars a week!
