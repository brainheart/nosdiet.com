Amber- The big thing I think is to make sure that when it comes time 
for your meals, that you are not UNDEREATING. I have a problem with 
this, from past diets, where I was so scared of eating beyond my 
satisfaction level, that I would not quite get to the point of eating 
to satisfaction... which meant a few hours later I was hungry yet 
again! If I recall in past posts here, I think the advice has been 
that when you are starting on nos, really load your meal plate 
initially so you are assured of not being hungry between meals, and 
once you have that down, you start to sort of naturally cut back on 
eating everything on your plate during mealtime. Does that make sense?
