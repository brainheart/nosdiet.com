Reinhard Engels <beautiful_idiot@...> wrote:
Thanks for the kind words, Bonnie, Kevin, Linda.

Kevin - I'm sorry your progress wasn't more
spectacular, but Linda is right: you have made
progress in terms of habits and self discipline, and
ultimately that's more important. Besides being less
sugar hungry, which I'm sure will pay dividends soon,
you've got a foundation of willpower on which to
build, and a much narrower range of suspects. Is it
big weekends that are the problem? Big firsts? Too
many exceptions? There are fewer variables. It's
easier to focus in now. You can fine tune.

As for exercise, I'd recommend just walking a lot
more. Not necessarily power walking or anything, but
just walking to places you ordinarily go instead of
driving or riding. It's useful, it's interesting, it's
kind of therapeutic, it's cheap, and you aren't going
to mess up your knees or anything.
