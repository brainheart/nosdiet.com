Kim--I do the last part of Slim in 6 (Burn It Up!) every other day
(this was after doing the workouts 6 days a week for 6 weeks).
Personally, I much prefer formal exercising every other day to 6 days
a week (I get the same results, anyway). I was thrilled when the 6-
week rotation was over at the beginning of June. I ordered the Slim
Series and plan on doing them every other day once I get them.

I feel that eating the NoS way gives better results than exercising
in terms of fat loss. Obviously, exercise has its benefits like
strengthening the heart and body. But overall, I feel that the
weight loss is achieved through dietary modifications. Back in the
days when I exercised 7 days a week (and didn't eat according to
NoS), I was heavier and carried more body fat than I do now.
