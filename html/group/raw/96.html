I agree with Brian. I just survived my second weekend on the no s diet, but when I got on my scales this morning, I gained back up to where I was when I started. that stinks cause I didn't overdo anything this weekend. I am going to take the advice of a wise person and put my scales in my garage. I feel lighter, am enjoying eating less, I think I look better, but all the scales do is depress me. Who needs that?!

At your age, (and any age) I think the best thing is to get active. Not only does it take our minds off food, but it raises the metabolism, and that's what really burns stored fat fast. I could go on and on about the benefits of exercise, but I won't. Just find something you like to do...and do it.
