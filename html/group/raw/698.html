Sorry it took me so long to give you an "official"
welcome, but it seems like you got plenty of the
unofficial kind.

There were a bunch of interesting posts about fruits
as snacks and quark-like substances. I'd just like to
clarify that:

1) Technically, no snacks means no snacks, fruit or
otherwise. I went through a phase where I was a little
cavalier about fruit, but I'm quite strict about it
now, and that's what I recommend. Strict doesn't
necessarily mean putting the fruit on same physical
plate (that's a little barbarous) but it means
reducing your other portions so that it *could* fit on
your plate. It's a great way to ensure that you eat
more good stuff and less bad stuff.

See:

http://www.nosdiet.com/#healthy_snacks

2) While I actually do like quark and such stuff, most
of what I eat is considerably more optimized towards
palatability. While you are welcome to, you don't
*have* to stick with arcane health foods on the
nosdiet. I welcome suggestions, and found this thread
quite interesting, but I just want to make sure people
don't get frightened off. Not everyone likes their
breakfast cheese redolent of quantum mechanics, and
that's ok.

Reinhard
