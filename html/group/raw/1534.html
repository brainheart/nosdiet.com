Hi everyone!

(I'm cross posting this to both lists, so apologies if you are subscribed to
both)

Today is my 3 week mark for nosdiet. I've dropped 4 pounds and can see a
visible difference on my face and waist. This is easily the best eating
regime I've ever been on, and (fortunately) it has not been a difficult one
for me.

I expected it to be very hard - I have a very sweet tooth. But I have been
surprised at how easy it has been to refuse sweets & snacks when offered me.
I have made very sure that each of my main meals have been enough to tide me
over for five hours. I make sure that I am satisfied at the end of each meal
(not 'full to overflowing', but comfortably satisfied) at it has worked!

I've also been shovelgloving every day (although not this week - eeek! I need
to get back in the saddle). I'm only using a 7lb hammer, and I didn't think
that I would ever get used to it, but just over a week into it and there was
no particular problems.

My SG routine is:

Drive Fenceposts 21-14-7
Churn Butter 14-7 (single arms)
Shovelling 21-14
No Name 14-7
Chop Wood 21-14
Hoist the Sack 14-7
Stoke the Oven 21-14
Tuck the Bales 21-14

As you can see, I am able to just get through the second circuit and start on
the third set of fenceposts when the 14 minutes are up. I'm able to do this
without sucking wind any more, although my forearms are nicely tight at the
end.

I find it very pleasant to fold my arms and think "yep - that's a bicep".
Never done that before!

I think I'll move up to a 10lb hammer in a few weeks. (I just got paid, so
I'll make a trip to Wickes).

I'm also Urban Rangering - about half an hour a day during the week, and then
one session of an hour to an hour and a half at the weekend (normally
Saturday). Every little helps!

Thanks Reinhard! These systems have been an absolute revelation!

DaveA
