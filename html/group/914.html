This is true. But here's the big advantage vs. the
regular way of eating pizza: people give you funny
looks when you do this (and if there's no one else
around, you're conscience will give you a funny look).
Take one slice at a time, and everyone loses track, it
doesn't seem that bad. But put them all in front of
you and self-deception becomes impossible. As I
mention on the site, "If the amount of food on your
one plate is a little bestial and disgusting, that's
part of the educational process."
