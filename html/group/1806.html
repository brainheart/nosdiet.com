Hey Jill :) This plan is really a no brainer...fortunately all the 
thinking and "brainwork" has been done for us...cold turkey sounds 

serious..I'm guessing you are a hardcore sugar junkie :)
If you concentrate on eliminating EXCESS sugar...ie: Soda, donuts, 
cake, and all the really high sugar stuff, during the week, even if 
you screw up occasionally (which I and others often do) you will 
still be cutting 75% of the crap that you would normally consume.
I don't recommend the all or nothing mentality here and I suggest 
that, unless sugar isn't a problem for you to begin with, keep two 
days where you are allowed a special treat...S days
No more "playing around for you" :) 
Hope NoS is fun and rewarding
