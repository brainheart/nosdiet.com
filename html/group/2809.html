Hi all,

I don't want to discourage posting, obviously that's
the whole point of this group. I just want to let
those who feel a little overwhelmed by the volume know
that a more manageable system is on the way, and that
they shouldn't tune out completely. The new system
should be even better for heavy posters/readers than
the current one, and make it possible for the more
time pressured to profitably skim in just a few
minutes a day (no more than 14!). Under the current
system, that's getting difficult, and will only get
more so as the group grows. 

In the meantime, post away if you're so inclined. Or
switch to "no email, web only" or "daily digest" if
it's too much for you. Deb recently posted
instructions on how to do this.

http://nosdiet.com/group/2802

Best to both camps (and the in between),
